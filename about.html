Our Mission

Welcome to Eco Sphere, an interactive platform designed to bridge the gap between environmental education and modern technology. Developed as part of our technical curriculum at SPPU, this project aims to raise awareness about global sustainability challenges through gamified experiences.

Why Eco Sphere?

Traditional learning can often feel abstract. Eco Sphere uses 3D visualization and Augmented Reality concepts to let users interact directly with environmental hazards and green solutions. By visualizing the impact of waste and pollution, we empower the next generation of eco-warriors to make informed decisions for our planet.

Visual Learning Real-time 3D models of environmental objects.
Interactive Logic Challenge your reasoning with our Eco-Scanner game.
AR Integration Immersive waste segregation mechanics.
